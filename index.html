Getting There

G

Reno Inter­national Airport

The Reno airport is a scenic 1 hour drive from Tahoe City, CA. Rental cars and shuttles are available at the airport. The next closest major airport is Sacramento, a two-hour drive through the Tahoe National Forest.

San Francisco Party Bus

For all of our guests coming from San Francisco, [person] has arranged for a weekend party bus to whisk you away to the Lake. The bus shuttle will leave Thursday afternoon and return to the city Sunday night.

Flying to SFO or OAK

If you're interested in enjoying the Bay Area before the wedding weekend, we recommend flying to SFO or OAK. The drive to Tahoe city will take 3-4 hours depending on traffic.
